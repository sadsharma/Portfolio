About Me

Hello! I'm Sadikshya Sharma or as everyone calls me, Sadie. I am a 19 year old aspiring software engineer and web developer.

Let's begin with a basic background of me! What we know so far: My name is Sadie and I'm 19. I am currently a sophmore in Stony Brook University. However, I plan to graduate in may 2024. My major is computer science and I truly love programming (but more on that later). I am originally from Nepal and moved to the United States when I was 10 years old. I absolutely love nepali food. And lastly, I'm going to point out the obvious as if I haven't shown it enough in this website, my favorite color is green.

Now that you basically know everything about me, onto the good stuff. Why do I love coding so much? The answer is "because you can do so much with so little". Take this website for example, this was my first project with HTMl, CSS or Javascript. I decided I wanted to make a personal website. Sat down with my laptop. Did alot of research and started typing away. All I needed was a laptop and I learned how to write code in three different languages and create art with javascript to make my website truly unique. Isn't that amazing?

As for my qualifications, click below to download my resume. I am very skilled at java and thoroughly enjoy coding in java. However, I am a fast learner (learned HTML CSS and Javascript to create this website in less than a month and a half) and would love to take on any language. I also have dabbled in game development using Unity and very much enjoyed that as well.

I'm going to stop myself here but if you would like to know more please reach out to me so we can talk! :)
